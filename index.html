Patent-Pending Document Scanner

Upload any FAR/BAR contract, addendum, or rider. AGENTA runs a two-pass analysis — the first pass extracts every date, party name, dollar amount, and contingency. The second pass cross-checks for inconsistencies, missing signatures, LLC single-signer issues, and other compliance flags. The whole process takes about 30 seconds. Seven provisional patents filed with the USPTO in April 2026.

🔔

Deadline Alert System

Every contractual deadline is tracked and alerted at three intervals — 7 days, 3 days, and 24 hours before it's due. Inspection periods, appraisal contingencies, HOA document deadlines, financing deadlines, closing dates. Florida business day computation is handled automatically — no manual calendar math, no miscounting weekends and holidays. You and your TC both get alerted.

📋

15-Item Compliance Checklist

Every transaction gets checked against a 15-item required document checklist — purchase agreement, addenda, disclosures, proof of funds, pre-approval, inspection report, appraisal, and more. AGENTA flags what's missing before your broker has to ask. Your file is always clean, always current, always ready for review.

✉️

Automated Client Updates

At every major milestone — offer accepted, inspection complete, appraisal in, clear to close, closing confirmed — AGENTA sends a professional client update email in your voice. Buyers and sellers feel informed without you having to draft another "just checking in" message. The update is milestone-triggered, not templated, so it always feels current and relevant.
